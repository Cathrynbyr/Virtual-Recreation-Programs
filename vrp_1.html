About Us

Mission Statement - Virtual Recreation Programs is an organization dedicated to improving the mental health and quality of life for senior citizens living in the community with the use of virtual recreational activities and educational programming.

We offer online art and crafting classes in a wide range of mediums that are both mentally stimulating and suitable for the beginner.

We offer a variety of low impact online exercise classes, yoga, tai chi, meditation, aromatherapy and spiritual programs. Online bingo, Pokeno, and trivia are also available for our clients.
